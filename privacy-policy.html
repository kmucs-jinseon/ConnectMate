Connect Mate Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-01

App name: Connect Mate

Developer: Jinseon Yoo

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application (“Connect Mate”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Your app collects only the minimum data required to operate its core features.

We collect the following categories of data:

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address
  • Username / Nickname
  • Profile photo

These are used for account creation, authentication, and profile display.

1.2 Messages & User Content

  • Messages or chat content
  • Photos or media uploaded by users

These are stored securely and used only for communication and matching features within the App.

1.3 Location Data

  • Approximate location

    Used to show relevant or nearby users, campuses, or matching results.

    The App does not collect precise GPS location.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect data through:

  • Account creation (Google, Naver, Kakao, or email/password signup)
  • User input (profile info, messages, images)
  • App usage (when you send messages or upload photos)
  • Device permissions (only when you choose to upload images)

We do not collect background location or any unnecessary data.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • App functionality

    To provide meal-matching, messaging, and social features.

  • Account management

    Login, identification, and profile display.

  • User-to-user communication

    Sending messages, viewing profiles, and chatting.

  • Personalization

    Showing your profile photo or nickname to others.

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third-party companies.

Data is only stored and processed by:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • GitHub Pages / GitHub DNS (hosting your privacy policy page)

These services act as processors on behalf of your App and do not use your data for their own purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data.

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Account data is stored only in Firebase Authentication.
  • Messages and user content are stored only in Firebase Realtime Database.

We take steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as your account is active.

When you delete your account, your personal information and chat content may be removed according to our future data-deletion system.

A “Data Deletion Request” feature will be implemented in a future update.

7. User Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights such as:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Request for deletion (will be available in the future)
  • Withdrawal of permissions

Currently:

  • You can delete your account in the App.
  • Full data-deletion requests will be supported soon.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

If minors (13–17) use the App, they should do so with guardian consent.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

If such data is found, we will delete it immediately.

9. Third-Party Services Used

The App uses the following services:

Firebase Authentication

Used for secure login, identity verification, and account management.

Firebase Realtime Database

Used to store messages, profile information, uploaded photos, and approximate location.

Login Providers

  • Google
  • Naver
  • Kakao

These providers only verify your identity; they do not receive your in-app data.

GitHub Pages (Optional)

Used to host this privacy policy page.

We do not use:

  • Ads SDKs
  • Analytics SDKs
  • Tracking SDKs
  • Device fingerprinting

10. Permissions Used

Depending on the features you use, the App may request:

  • Photo Library Access (when uploading images)
  • Location Access (approximate or precise)

We do not access:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Phone number

    Unless explicitly stated.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If significant changes are made, we will notify users within the App or through an update notice.
